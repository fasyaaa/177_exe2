BACKBURNER

"Backburner" by NIKI is a song from Nicole's album which tells the story of regret and introspection in an ending relationship. In her lyrics, NIKI conveys feelings of sadness and self-reflection after realizing that the relationship was not going as expected. With a gentle melody and soulful vocals, "Backburner" depicts the complexity of emotions experienced when facing the end of a love story.
